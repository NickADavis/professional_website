The content of the Henderson Inverse Theorem is as follows: Two systems with Hamiltonians of the form,

$$
  \mathcal{H} = \sum_i \frac{p_i^2}{2m} + \frac{1}{2} \sum_{i \neq j} u(|\mathbf{r_i} - \mathbf{r_j}|)
$$
